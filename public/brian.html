Why I built this

Most people don’t need a perfect plan — they need a tiny action that creates momentum. Hit Your Day is designed to be frictionless: start, move, done.

What I believe:

  • Consistency beats intensity
  • Start small, show up daily
  • Movement is the fastest way to change state
  • Privacy and simplicity aren’t “nice-to-haves” — they’re the product

Founder FAQ

Is Hit Your Day a boxing program?

Not exactly — it’s a daily momentum habit using simple shadowboxing movement. No equipment, no technique required.

Do I need experience?

Nope. If you can throw basic punches toward the camera, you can do it.

What’s the long-term vision?

Build a frictionless daily practice that keeps people consistent — and unlock deeper training for those who want it (without ruining the simplicity).
