FAQ

Do I need to know how to box?

Not at all! Just throw punches at the camera. No experience, equipment, or technique required.

What is Hit Your Day?

A frictionless daily habit. Throw punches for a short round, hit your goal, and build a streak.

Do you store my camera video?

We don't store your raw camera feed. After your round, we may generate a short shareable clip (about 5 seconds) so you can download or share it. We then delete the video so nothing is saved on our side.

How do I keep my streak across devices?

Enter your email after a round to link your streak to you (so it follows you on any device).
