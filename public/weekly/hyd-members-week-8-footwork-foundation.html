Members Only • Week 8

Footwork

This week is about learning how to move with balance, control, and confidence. We’re focusing on staying in stance while stepping, creating angles, and learning how good footwork sets up everything in boxing.

30-Minute Session
Movement + Technique
Equipment: 1-3lb handweights

Theme of the Week

Footwork is what keeps you balanced, mobile, and in control. This week is about learning how to move without crossing your feet, stay in stance while stepping, and use movement to create better boxing positions.

Main Focus

Stay in stance. Take small steps. Move with control.

Session Objective

By the end of this session, you should feel more comfortable moving forward, backward, side to side, and resetting your stance while staying balanced.

What to Remember

  • Stay in your boxing stance
  • Take small controlled steps
  • Never cross your feet
  • Move the foot closest to the direction first
  • Stay balanced and ready to punch

Warmup • 5 Minutes

1 minute

Light bounce in boxing stance

1 minute

Forward and backward step drills

1 minute

Side-to-side movement in stance

2 minutes with handweights

Light shadowboxing while moving

Technique Focus • Footwork

Key Coaching Points

  • Keep your knees soft and slightly bent
  • Stay light on the balls of your feet
  • Move with small steps instead of big jumps
  • Push off the opposite foot when moving
  • Reset your stance after every movement

Common Mistakes

  • Crossing your feet while moving
  • Standing too tall
  • Taking steps that are too big
  • Letting your feet come too close together
  • Losing stance before or after punching

Main Training • 15 Minutes

Round 1 — Forward and Backward Movement

Step in, step out, and reset your stance every time.

Round 2 — Side-to-Side Movement

Move left and right while staying balanced and controlled.

Round 3 — Step + Jab

Move first, then throw the jab while keeping your feet underneath you.

Round 4 — Step Back + Cross

Create distance with your feet, then fire the rear hand.

Round 5 — Freestyle Movement Round

Mix punches with movement in all directions while staying relaxed and balanced.

Strength Block • 5 Minutes

  • 20 squats
  • 20 calf raises
  • 30 mountain climbers
Complete 2 rounds with minimal rest.

Why the Strength Work Matters

  • Squats improve balance and leg strength
  • Calf raises build foot and ankle stability
  • Mountain climbers improve speed, control, and endurance
